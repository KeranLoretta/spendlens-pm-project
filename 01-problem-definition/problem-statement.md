#Problem Statment
Young professionals and students in India who regularly use UPI payment apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm lack meaningful, automatic expense insights. 
They can view transaction history, but they cannot easily understand where their money is going, how much they spend in each category, or whether they are staying within budget.

As a result, users rely on manual tracking, feel less financially aware, overspend more easily, and struggle to build savings habits. 
This also weakens long-term engagement with the app because the product helps users make payments, but not manage money.

This problem is especially relevant for 18–30 year olds, a segment that uses UPI heavily and is highly sensitive to convenience, financial visibility, and budgeting support
